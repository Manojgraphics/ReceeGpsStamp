Recce GPS Stamp — Privacy Policy

By Manoj Graphics (Media Earth), Jalgaon · Last updated 25 June 2026

Recce GPS Stamp (“the app”) is an offline-first, GPS-stamp camera for field surveys and branding recces. This policy explains what the app accesses and how your data is handled.

What the app stores

Shop details you enter (name, contact, city, status, remarks), the photos you capture, and the GPS location stamped on them. All of this is stored only on your device. The app works fully offline and we do not collect this data on any server of ours.

Permissions used

  • Camera — to take shop-front and media photos.
  • Location (GPS) — to stamp coordinates & address onto photos, only while capturing.
  • Photos / Storage — to save the stamped photos to your gallery.

Google sign-in & Google Drive (optional)

Sign-in is optional — the app works fully without it. If you sign in with Google, it is only to identify you and to back up to your own Google Drive.

Drive access uses the limited drive.file scope: the app can only see and manage files it creates (the “Recce GPS Stamp” backup folder). It cannot read or touch your other Drive files, photos or documents. Backups go only to your own Drive — we never store your photos or data on our servers.

Sharing

You choose when to share reports (WhatsApp / PDF / Excel). The app never sends anything automatically.

Data deletion

Uninstall the app to remove local data. Delete the “Recce GPS Stamp” folder from your Google Drive to remove backups.
